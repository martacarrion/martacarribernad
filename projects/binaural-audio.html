Binaural Audio — Wishes for 2026

Audiovisual project exploring binaural sound recording in a public environment. Using a binaural microphone head, we interviewed people in Plaza del Ayuntamiento about their wishes for the year 2026.

The project highlights the diversity of hopes and concerns shared by citizens, while the binaural audio recording allows the viewer to experience an immersive 360° sound environment.

Technologies

  • Binaural audio recording
  • Field interviews
  • Video editing
  • Spatial audio
